 Social Media Checklist for College Students

The DO's:-

 1) Use a professional profile picture on LinkedIn and GitHub. 
 2) Share your projects, certifications, and academic wins. 
 3) Check your privacy settings regularly on personal accounts. 
 4) Network with alumni and professionals in your field. 
 5) Use Two-Factor Authentication(2FA) to secure your accounts. 

The DON'Ts:-
 1) Post confidential information about your school or internship. 
 2) Engage in heated digital arguments or "flame wars." 
 3) Post photos involving illegal activities or policy violations. 
 4) Share unverified news or "fake news" without fact-checking. 
 5) Badmouth professors, peers, or previous employers online.
